Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up / Register?

If you are not registered with us, you can see "Become a Donor" button in Home page.

Can I register two times?

No you can't register two times. You can update your profile at any time.

How can I edit/update my details after registration?

Goto Home page and click three dot menu

Then goto Profile

Here you can see "Update Donor Details" button

Can I change my Registered phone number?

Yes, you can change your phone number at any time.

Can I update my Name after registration?

No, you can't update your Name, Blood Group and Gender after registration.

Can I remove my donor account?

No you can't delete your donor account right now.
